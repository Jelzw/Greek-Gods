Zeus

Zeus, the God of Thunder is the king of all Greek Gods and one of the 12 Olympians who live in Mount Olympus. Zeus is also part of the big three and plays a significant role in Greek Mythology.

Poseidon

Poseidon, God of the Sea, is also one of the 12 Olympians and also a part of the big three. Known for his trident and power over the seas, Poseidon has a significant role in the mythology of ancient Greece.

Hades

Hades, God of the underworld and the dead, he is famous for owning the three-headed dog Cerberus. He is also a part of the big three along with Zeus and Poseidon.
